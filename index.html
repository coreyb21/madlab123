Cleverly done, Mr Freeman, but you're not supposed to be here.

A website dedicated to detailing information about the Half Life 2 Beta leak.

The right man in the wrong place can make all the difference in the world.

On October 7th 2003, the files for Valve's upcoming game, Half Life 2, were leaked to the public. Because of this leak, we were able to discover the disturbing world being conjured up by Valve. Most notable differences from retail include major changes in atmosphere and cut enemies.
